Editorial Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Purpose of This Policy

IPL Auction publishes educational content to help users understand mock cricket auctions, room setup, and bidding strategy. This policy explains how our content is created, reviewed, updated, and corrected.

What We Publish

Our content includes practical guides, FAQs, setup tutorials, and strategy explainers for simulated IPL-style auctions. We do not publish official IPL governance announcements or represent ourselves as an official league authority.

Source and Verification Standards

  • 1. We verify game-rule explanations against platform behavior and current room settings.
  • 2. Feature descriptions are checked against live production pages before publication.
  • 3. Educational strategy content is reviewed for clarity and consistency, not presented as guaranteed outcomes.
  • 4. Time-sensitive details are revised when significant product or season changes happen.

Originality and Value Standards

We aim to publish practical, original, and user-focused material. We avoid publishing low-information pages, duplicate summaries, and generic filler that does not help users complete tasks.

  • 1. Each page must solve a clear user need (how to host, configure, bid, or troubleshoot).
  • 2. Pages are expected to include actionable instructions, examples, or decision guidance.
  • 3. Internal links are used to connect readers to related high-value pages.

AI and Automation Disclosure

We may use writing assistance tools during drafting, but all published content is reviewed and edited before release. We do not intentionally publish unreviewed auto-generated text.

Correction Policy

If we identify a factual or instructional error, we update the page and revise the "Last updated" date. Users can report issues via the contact page, and priority corrections are handled first for errors that affect gameplay guidance.

Advertising and Editorial Independence

Advertising does not control our instructions, strategy recommendations, or support content. We keep educational material separate from ad placements.
